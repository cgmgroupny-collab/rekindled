# Vivienne — Marketing Plan for Immediate Sales

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Women 45-65, in relationships or recently separated, English & Spanish speaking
- **Secondary:** Men 45-65 buying gifts for partners, couples seeking reconnection
- **Psychographics:** Readers of self-help/relationship books, followers of Esther Perel, Brene Brown, Melody Beattie. Value depth over surface. Willing to invest in personal growth.

---

## PHASE 1: WEEK 1-2 — Launch & Viral Quiz (Cost: $0)

### 1. Quiz Viral Loop (PRIMARY DRIVER)
The "How's Your Eroticism?" quiz is the #1 growth engine.

**Strategy:**
- Share quiz link on all personal social accounts
- Ask 20 friends/contacts to take & share (DM each personally)
- Post quiz results as Instagram/Facebook carousels: "I got Dormant Flame — what's yours?"
- Create shareable result images for each of the 4 types (Dormant Flame, Controlled Fire, Curious Spark, Living Flame)

**Viral mechanics:**
- Quiz results create FOMO + curiosity ("what did YOU get?")
- Results are non-judgmental — people WANT to share
- Email capture at the end converts quiz-takers to leads
- Each result includes a CTA to the journal or sessions

**Target: 500 quiz completions in Week 1, 30% email capture = 150 leads**

### 2. Instagram Launch (Organic)
**Account:** @askvivienne or @vivienne.app

**Content calendar (3 posts/day for 14 days):**
- Morning: Daily reflection quote card (Beattie/Perel quotes on warm backgrounds using our generated images)
- Afternoon: Carousel teaching one concept (erotic intelligence, letting go, play, etc.)
- Evening: Story poll / question sticker ("When was the last time you played with your partner?")

**Reels (1/day):**
- 30-60 second clips reading the daily reflection in a warm voice
- "Vivienne asks:" prompt videos — pose a question, let comments blow up
- Behind-the-scenes of building the brand

**Hashtags:** #relationshipwisdom #estherperel #lettinggo #couplesover40 #loveafter50 #eroticintelligence #innerwork #melodybeattie #secondact #relationshipgoals

### 3. Facebook Groups (Organic, High-Intent)
Join and contribute value in these groups (DO NOT SPAM — add value first):
- Esther Perel fan groups
- "Where Should We Begin" podcast listeners
- Melody Beattie / Codependency recovery groups
- "Relationships After 40/50" groups
- "Divorce Recovery" groups
- "Second Marriage" groups

**Strategy:** Answer questions genuinely. After 5-7 value posts, share the quiz as a "free tool I found helpful." Link to Vivienne naturally.

### 4. Email List Building
- Set up ConvertKit or Mailchimp free tier
- Daily reflection email (automated sequence, 30 days)
- Welcome email → Quiz CTA → Journal CTA → Session CTA (nurture funnel)
- Each email ends with "P.S. Forward this to someone who needs it today"

## PHASE 2: WEEK 3-4 — First Revenue ($200-500 budget)

### 5. Facebook/Instagram Ads (Paid)
**Budget:** $10-15/day = $200-300/month

**Ad #1 — Quiz Ad (Top of Funnel)**
- Image: hero-couple.png or hands-intimate.png
- Headline: "How's Your Eroticism? Take the free quiz"
- Copy: "7 questions inspired by Esther Perel. Discover your relationship pattern — are you a Dormant Flame or a Living Flame?"
- CTA: Take the Quiz
- Targeting: Women 45-65, interests: Esther Perel, relationship books, self-improvement, Brene Brown, Oprah
- **Expected:** $0.50-1.50 per quiz completion

**Ad #2 — Journal Ad (Middle of Funnel, retarget quiz takers)**
- Image: journal-product.png
- Headline: "The Inner Work Journal — 30 days to deeper love"
- Copy: "Guided prompts, letting go meditations, and 52 conversation cards for couples. By Vivienne."
- CTA: Get for $14.99
- Targeting: Retarget quiz completers + email list
- **Expected:** 3-5% conversion = 5-15 sales/week = $75-225/week

**Ad #3 — Session Ad (Bottom of Funnel, retarget journal buyers)**
- Image: vivienne-portrait.png
- Headline: "90 minutes with Vivienne. $249."
- Copy: "Deep relationship audit, personalized action plan, 7-day follow-up. Includes the journal free."
- CTA: Book Session
- Targeting: Journal buyers + high-intent quiz takers
- **Expected:** 1-2 bookings/month = $249-498/month

### 6. Pinterest (Free, Long-term SEO)
- Create pins for each daily reflection quote (use our warm images as backgrounds)
- Pin the quiz, journal, and gift shop items
- Pinterest skews 40+ female — PERFECT audience
- Boards: "Relationship Wisdom," "Letting Go Quotes," "Gifts for Him/Her," "Erotic Intelligence"
- **Expected:** Slow build, 500-2000 monthly visitors after 60 days

### 7. Affiliate Gift Shop Revenue
- Sign up for Amazon Associates, Uncommon Goods affiliate, Nordstrom affiliate
- Replace placeholder gift links with real affiliate links
- Create "Gift Guide" blog post / Pinterest board
- Average commission: 4-8% = $5-40 per sale
- **Expected:** $100-300/month passive once traffic hits 1000+/month
