Common Questions
Everything you need to know

Who is Vivienne for?

Adults (40+) who want deeper, more alive relationships. Whether you're in a long-term partnership, recently separated, or single and preparing for your next chapter.

Is this therapy?

No. Vivienne is a wisdom companion, not a therapist. She draws from the frameworks of Esther Perel and Melody Beattie to guide self-reflection and growth. For clinical issues, please see a licensed professional.

What is "erotic intelligence"?

As Esther Perel defines it: the quality of imagination, curiosity, playfulness, mystery, and risk-taking in your relationships. It's about aliveness, not just sex.

How are sessions conducted?

90-minute sessions via Zoom or phone. You'll receive a pre-session questionnaire, the session recording, a summary with action items, and 7 days of email follow-up.

Can I gift a session or journal?

Absolutely! Both make beautiful gifts. After purchase, you'll receive a printable gift certificate you can personalize.

Is the content available in Spanish?

Yes! The full site and daily reflections are available in Spanish. Click "ES" in the navigation to switch. Sessions can be conducted in English or Spanish.
